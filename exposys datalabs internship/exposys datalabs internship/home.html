TECHNOLOGY

IT, biotechnology, aerospace, nuclear science, manufacturing technology, automobile engineering, chemical engineering, ship building, space science, electronics, computer science and other medical science related research and development are occurring on a large scale in the country

ACHIVEMENTS

Ancient India was home to two of the world's first cities, Harappa and Mohenjo-Daro. These cities had stone buildings, multiply stories, and sewage systems! India was one of the very first civilizations to use algebra and calculus. The number zero was invented in Ancient India by a man named Aryabhatta.

Launching of the Indian Constittuon, eradication of polio, developing of green revolution, right to education for everyone, innovation of science and technology are the 5 achievements of India.
